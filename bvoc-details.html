Program Details

Everything you need to know about the B.Voc program structure, eligibility, and admission process.

Eligibility Criteria

Basic Requirements

  • 10+2 or equivalent from any recognized board
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks (45% for reserved categories)
  • Mathematics/Computer Science at 10+2 level (for Software Development)
  • Age: 17-25 years as of course commencement

Additional Requirements

  • Basic computer literacy
  • English language proficiency
  • Aptitude for chosen specialization
  • Interview clearance (if applicable)
